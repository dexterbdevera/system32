WHO WE ARE

System32 is a 4 piece punk, metal, alternative rock band from the Philippines that is currently based in Singapore. The band was formed in 2017 and is composed of IT Professionals working in the Lion city, with sundry backgrounds of musical influences and individually rich experiences in live music performances on stage.

Our line up includes Marco Desdir as Lead Singer and Rythym guitarist, Dexter De Vera as Lead guitarist, backing vocals, Marwin Muega on Bass Guitar and Yuri Pangco on Drums.
